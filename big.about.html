KiU Generation is a leading travel company specializing in organizing unique and unforgettable journeys across Kyrgyzstan. Our goal is to showcase the true beauty and richness of this amazing country, where centuries-old traditions, majestic mountains, and breathtaking natural landscapes converge.

About our company

The company has earned recognition as one of the best tour operators in the region thanks to its high level of service and environmentally conscious approach to tourism. We take pride in participating in international initiatives for preserving natural heritage and supporting local communities, as well as organizing journeys for renowned explorers and photographers.

About Our Employees

Our team of professionals works with a love for Kyrgyzstan and attention to every traveler. We strive to ensure that trips with "KiU" inspire you to return, discovering new facets of this amazing country.

We specialize in

The company's specialization covers a wide range of tourism types. These include eco-tours to the natural gems of Kyrgyzstan, trekking and mountaineering in the Tien Shan and Pamir mountains, ethnological tours immersing travelers in the life of nomadic peoples, as well as cultural routes that reveal the rich history of the region. Additionally, "KiU" offers active leisure activities such as horseback riding, rafting, and bike expeditions.
